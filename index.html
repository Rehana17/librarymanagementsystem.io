    Rental Rules


    • Books can be rented for a maximum period of 30 days.
    • Late returns will incur a fine of ₹1 per day.
    • Books should be handled with care to avoid damage or loss.
    • Any damage to a rented book must be reported immediately.
    • Highlighting, underlining, or writing in the books is strictly prohibited.
    • Books should be returned in the same condition as they were rented.
    • Lost or damaged books will result in a replacement fee equal to the book's value.
    • Books can be renewed for an additional rental period if no other users have requested them.
    • Returning books beyond the rental period without prior notice may affect future borrowing privileges.

    Returning Books

    When returning a book, please follow these steps:

    • Ensure the book is in good condition with no damages or markings.
    • Place the book in a protective cover or bag to prevent any further damage.
    • Return the book to the library's designated return area or desk.
    • Check with library staff to ensure the return is recorded and any fines are cleared.
